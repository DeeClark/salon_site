We are a full service salon serving northeastern Kansas. We are proud to offer a 'green' line of beauty and heath products, our Aveda© line is over 90% natural. We have been in business since 1992, and are happy to provide friendly service and quality products to Manhattan, Wamego, St. Marys and the surrounding cities.

Our Mission

Our mission always has been, and always will be about quality, which inspires and nurtures the human spirit. Our work is never done because we are always trying to improve the lives of people. Clients and staff are treated with respect and dignity. We make it s point to fully engage, connect, laugh with, and uplift everyone’s spirit. Our salon is a place to connect with friends and enjoy the sense of belonging. We strive to achieve the highest possible standards in all that we do. Our number one priority is about commitment, and to give our clients competitive creativity at the highest of our abilities. As an Aveda salon, we follow what nature has created, and we ensure that every client leaves not only looking beautiful, but also feeling beautiful.
